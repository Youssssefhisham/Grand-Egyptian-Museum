Policies and Guidelines for Visitors

As part of our commitment to visitors and providing an enjoyable museum experience, please adhere to the visit regulations and guidelines to maintain the museum environment and its exhibits, as well as to ensure a safe and pleasant visit.

To protect the exhibits and artworks, please kindly adhere to the following recommendations

  • Large bags, large backpacks, travel bags, or carry-on bags exceeding 40*40 cm in dimensions are not allowed inside the museum. Please deposit them at the museum's storage.
  • Museum staff are permitted to inspect the contents of bags and backpacks. Sharp, piercing, or dangerous objects are not allowed inside the museum (such as knives, screwdrivers, scissors, weapons), which must be deposited at the personal storage.
  • Do not bring umbrellas inside the museum; please store them at the storage.
  • Do not bring folding chairs or any type of seats into the exhibition halls without permission from museum security.
  • Musical instruments are not allowed inside the museum.
  • Pets are not allowed...
  • Advertising boards, banners, or logos are not allowed inside the museum without obtaining necessary approvals from the relevant authorities.

General visit guidelines inside exhibition halls

  • Please do not touch the exhibits or display cases.
  • Food and drinks are not allowed inside exhibition halls (small water bottles are permitted).
  • Smoking is prohibited throughout the museum.
  • Please do not engage in any illegal acts, vandalism, profanity, or unacceptable behavior.
  • Please be considerate of others and maintain quiet in the museum, especially when using mobile phones.
  • The use of sound amplifiers is not permitted anywhere in the museum.
  • Please do not use flashlights or laser pointers inside the museum to ensure the safety of exhibits.
  • Please maintain appropriate appearance...
  • Please do not lie on seats or the floor, or remove shoes in the museum.
  • Please follow directional signs.

Families accompanying children

  • Children must be accompanied by their guardians inside the museum at all times.
  • For the safety of your children and the museum exhibits, please prevent children
    from running or colliding with exhibits or other visitors, which may cause injuries or damage.

Photography

  • Personal photography and video are permitted inside the museum after purchasing a photography ticket.
  • Photographing other visitors and museum staff is prohibited as it violates their personal rights.
  • Photography and video for commercial purposes (TV, cinema, programs, advertisements, documentaries, etc.) are permitted after obtaining prior permission from the relevant authorities and paying the specified fee.
  • The use of flash is prohibited when taking photos.
  • The use of tripods or monopods is prohibited except for commercial photography.

Drawing

Fine arts college students are requested to adhere to the following:

  • Ink pens are prohibited in all exhibition halls; only pencils are allowed for drawing or writing.
  • Drawing pads should not exceed 24*18 inches.
  • Please do not block visitor pathways in exhibition halls while drawing.

In emergency situations:

  • Please follow museum staff instructions during emergencies (earthquakes, fires).
  • Please stay away from large pieces, display cases, and any exhibits that may fall during an earthquake.
